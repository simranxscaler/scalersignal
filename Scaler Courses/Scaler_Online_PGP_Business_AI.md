## The Journey

**5 Phases. 12 Months. From Capable to Unstoppable.**

### Phase 1: Business & Tech Foundations *(2 Months)*

Reset how you think. Master data analysis, economics, unit economics, simple P&Ls. Build your foundation so everything that follows clicks.

**What you walk away with:** You can read a business like a founder. You see the numbers. You see the levers.

---

### Phase 2: Management Mastery *(4 Months)*

Go deep on strategy, finance, marketing, operations, leadership. Porter's 5 Forces. Unit economics. Customer journeys. Org design. You're learning the language of CXOs, not just remembering frameworks.

**What you walk away with:** You can think and contribute like a business operator.

---

### Phase 3: AI Foundation *(1 Month)*

Learn how AI thinks — transformers, embeddings, multimodal models — and master task design, observability, evals, and early agentic workflows for safe, reliable execution.

**What you walk away with:** You become AI-fluent, not just AI-aware.

---

### Phase 4: AI in Business Specialization *(3 Months)*

Now apply AI across product, marketing, finance, operations, and strategy. Learning to think and execute AI-first in every business function.

**What you walk away with:** AI becomes part of how you work and make every decision.

---

### Phase 5: Industry Capstone *(2 Months)*

Take a real problem from one of our 1200+ partner companies. Ship a solution. Build your portfolio. Walk away with a flagship project that proves you can do this.

**What you walk away with:** A project you can show. Proof you can deliver.
