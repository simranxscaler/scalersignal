## Learning Pedagogy

### Onboarding Session
- **Course Introduction** — Start in a customised cohort and build connections with peers who'll be your allies throughout
- **Choose Your Mentor** — Select the right mentor for career guidance from the first day
- **Meet a Learning Coordinator** — Ensures seamless progress, support, and communication throughout the course

### Live Learning Experience
- Learn in a **live classroom** — engage with instructors and peers in real time for diverse perspectives and instant clarifications
- **Assignments & Homeworks** — Deepen understanding by applying techniques from class
- **1:1 Mentor Guidance** — Personalised, tailored support through every doubt
- **AI-Assisted Problem-Solving** — Recommendations and solutions across a spectrum of challenges
- **1:1 Teaching Assistance** — Live chat and video call to resolve individual queries
- **AI Mock Interviews** — Practice for Platform Engineer, SRE, and Cloud Architect roles with live avatar interviews built around what teams are actually hiring for

Hands-on learning by solving **50+ real case studies** that include datasets from top tech companies. Examples include creating custom Docker images and configuring sudo privileges in live terminal environments.
